Technical Interview Breakdown (25 min)

10 mins
Student Technical Breakdown
What is your technical background?
This gives us a sense of how long they have been interested in the technology industry.
What technologies have you researched up to this point?
Have they been doing self research up to this point? Key words to listen for "javascript", "html", "css", "python", "c", "c#", "java"
Have you used any libraries or frameworks. If so which ones?
If they have not that is ok. This is to determine how advanced the student might be. If they have used React and Node in the past we will want to dive deeper into their understanding to make sure they are not to advanced for the immersive.
When you run into a bug how do you go about solving the problem?
Key things to listen for: "take a break and come back", "console log", "look on stack overflow", "do additional research", "MDN documentation"
How do you take care of yourself when you are under stress?
Due to the stress of the cohort we want to make sure the student knows how to handle stress.
